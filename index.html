About Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its art, fashion, and culture. The city is home to iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum, which houses thousands of works of art.

Top Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Champs-Élysées
  • Montmartre

Cuisine

Paris is renowned for its exquisite cuisine. Don’t miss trying local dishes like croissants, escargots, and coq au vin. Pair your meals with some fine French wine for a complete experience.
